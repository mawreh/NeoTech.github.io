Introducing Imperium

Imperium is a specialized storage device capable of securely containing an instance of artificial intelligence (A.I.). Brought to you by NeoTech, the technology utilizes state-of-the-art cybersecurity software to ensure that the storage drive is 100% self contained, and that no data can be transferred to or from the device without an explicit hardware connection and the proper credentials to access the device in the first place.

Approx. 95% of Imperium's salability in hardware, making material usage (and waste) maximumly flexible and environmentally amicable.

Outer coating of product made from recycled plastic meaning Imperium can decompose in landfills after < 30 years.
